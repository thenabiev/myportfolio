About Me

Hello, my name is Sahin. I have been developing myself in web programming for more than a year. I decided to focus on this field as I found front end programming more suitable for me. I have been eager to learn new things since I was a child. For example, in order to find out what was inside my toys, I took them apart and collected them again :) and sometimes I was upset because I couldn't collect them. That's when I realized that great experiences are gained by making a few mistakes. Later, I grew up, toys were replaced by books. I was admitted to the university I always dreamed of. During those years, I tried myself in many fields, 3d modeling, graphic design, etc. However, after receiving a master's degree, I got to know people working in this field. Later, I started to study this field in depth. I am currently pursuing my master's degree and learning new things every day. That's all I have to say about me, you can contact me to get to know me better. See you soon...
